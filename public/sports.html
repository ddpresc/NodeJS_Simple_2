Friendly Foodie Recipe #1

Taco Salad

  • 1 pound Ground Beef
  • 1 packet Taco Seasoning Mix
  • Lettuce
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Tortilla chips

  • 1 pound of ground beef is enough for 6 servings. You can use whatever amount you want for the salad ingredients.

  • Brown 1 pound of ground beef until it is no longer pink.
  • Drain the grease.
  • Add 1 packet of taco seasoning mix following directions on packet.
  • Crush tortilla chips and set them aside in a bowl.
  • Add lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and crushed tortilla chips to a bowl.
  • Add the ground beef mixture to the salad.
  • Enjoy!
